The Challenge

Entering a crowded market requires more than just a good product; it requires a narrative that compels an audience to invest upfront. The goal was to build a comprehensive marketing analysis and strategy to support a successful crowdfunding launch for BeeAlp.

The Approach

I started by mapping the target consumer ecosystem. By understanding where potential backers spent their time and what messaging resonated with them, I was able to structure a multi-channel IMC strategy. This ensured consistent messaging across social media, email marketing, and PR leading up to the launch.

The Solution

The final deliverable was a phased strategy roadmap. It detailed pre-launch audience building tactics, mid-campaign momentum drivers, and post-campaign retention strategies, ensuring BeeAlp had a clear playbook for sustained growth.
