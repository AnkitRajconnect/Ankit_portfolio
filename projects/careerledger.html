CareerLedger

Marketing Director — Strategy, Acquisition, Lifecycle

CareerLedger is a SaaS platform helping professionals track and manage their career progression. I was brought in to architect the full marketing function from scratch — building a growth engine that spanned organic search, paid acquisition, email lifecycle, and retention strategy.

Key Results

  • 5,000+ new leads in the first 3 months through organic channels alone
  • Reduced customer acquisition cost (CAC) by 38% through funnel optimisation
  • Built a reporting dashboard tracking performance across 6 channels
  • Designed and launched full email lifecycle: onboarding, nurture, and re-engagement flows

The Challenge

CareerLedger launched with a strong product but no marketing infrastructure. The task was to build everything from zero — define the ICP, establish channel strategy, and start driving growth without burning budget on channels that weren't proven.

Strategy & Approach

I started with a deep audit of the competitive landscape and keyword opportunity. Rather than spray-and-pay with paid ads, I built an organic-first content strategy targeting high-intent career management queries. This gave us compounding traffic while paid channels were being tested and optimised in parallel.

Execution

On the paid side, I launched tightly-themed Google Ads campaigns with SKAGs structure, A/B tested landing page variants, and systematically cut underperforming ad groups. On the email side, I segmented the user base by acquisition source and lifecycle stage, building tailored sequences for each cohort.

Results

The organic strategy became our most cost-effective acquisition channel within 60 days. The email lifecycle improved 30-day retention by over 20%. The reporting dashboard gave leadership full visibility into channel-level ROI for the first time.
