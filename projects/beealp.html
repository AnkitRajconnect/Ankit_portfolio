The Challenge

The European smartphone market has 85%+ penetration. Consumers are suffering from replacement fatigue, yet massive incumbents dominate attention. BeeAlp's circular premium subscription needed an Integrated Marketing Communications (IMC) campaign to validate the model and secure €500k in seed crowdfunding.


The AIDA Digital Funnel

To convert a cold audience into backers, I structured a €45,000 digital strategy across four precise phases:

  • 1. Awareness: Leveraging LinkedIn and X to spark debate on digital sovereignty and the circular economy.
  • 2. Interest: Educational IG Carousels breaking down the lifetime cost comparison vs. traditional ownership.
  • 3. Desire: Community building via Discord and targeted email flows to showcase the "Phone as Mine" customizable OS.
  • 4. Action: High-urgency crowdfunding launch videos and early-bird conversion tactics.

The Content Pillars

We translated complex tech specs into five core consumer benefits to drive the social calendar:

  1. Premium Continuity: Focusing on 8-year performance, proving "Year 3 is still fast."
  2. Phone as Mine: Highlighting UI personalization saved to the cloud.
  3. Care Included: Publicizing repair transparency and iFixit partnerships.
  4. Smart Economics: Marketing the declining subscription price model.
  5. Circular Proof: Delivering factual sustainability without being preachy.
